Question 9: Again, pretend you are the instructor. Do you think it was helpful or not helpful to make this survey anonymous? Why or why not?
Answer: I think making the survey anonymous had both pros and cons.
Pro: Students may have been more honest about their experience levels and current status if they knew their instructor could not identify them. This gives more accurate data overall.
Con: Because the survey was anonymous, it is harder to follow up with specific students who might need extra help. For example, if a student has zero Excel experience, the instructor cannot reach out to offer additional support without knowing who that student is. Overall, a partially anonymous survey where names are collected but kept private might have been more useful.
